Himagsikan

About

A week-long game jam developed with my team 'StudioEepy', to design digital games inspired by Bicol mythology, resulting in a PvE multiplayer survival roguelite action game featuring characters from Bicol Mythology.

Project Info

Team Size: 6
Time frame: 4 Weeks
Engine: Unity (C#)

Introduction

I worked with my Team Studio Eepy at Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology (BISCAST) and join Mythical Bicol Game Jam last November 2025, our challenge was to developed a game that inspired by Bicol Mythology and Folkore. I was part of a team of 6 members and assigned as the Game Artist of the project. Our goal was to create a 3D PvE multiplayer survival roguelite action game featuring characters from Bicol Mythology.

My Role and Contributions

As the Game Artist, I was responsible for designing and creating the visual elements of the game. This included character models, UI components, Logo and Poster Design. I worked closely with the rest of the team to ensure that the art style was consistent and aligned with the game's theme.

Dayo

About

An endless first-person action/arcade shooter where the player defends a home from waves of dark entities. Integrates AI-driven enemy movement, resource management, and survival-based progression inspired by Storm the House and Spectral. Features adaptive behavior and randomized loot drops.

Project Info

Team Size: 1
Time frame: 4 Weeks
Engine: Unity (C#)

Introduction

Dayo is a 3D first-person endless action/arcade shooter game that I developed solo using Unity. In this game, players take on the role of a defender protecting their home from waves of dark entities. The game integrates AI-driven enemy movement, resource management, and survival-based progression inspired by games like Storm the House and Spectral. It features adaptive enemy behavior and randomized loot drops to keep players engaged.

Core Mechanics & Features

The core mechanics of Dayo revolve around wave-based combat. Players must strategically defend their home by utilizing a variety of weapons while managing limited resources. The game features a dynamic wave system that increases in difficulty over time, challenging players to adapt their strategies.

That Ain't Scrap Metal

About

A fast-paced 2D action endless game developed with my team 'StudioEepy', where a lone engineer defends a facility’s energy core from relentless scavenger robots using upgraded weapons and collected scrap.

Project Info

Team Size: 4
Time frame: 3 weeks
Engine: Unity (C#)

Introduction

Working as the Game Designer for 'That Ain't Scrap Metal', I collaborated with Studio Eepy a team of four to create a fast-paced 2D endless action game. In this game, players assume the role of a lone engineer tasked with defending a facility's energy core from relentless waves of scavenger robots. The game emphasizes quick reflexes and strategic resource management, as players collect scrap to upgrade their weapons and enhance their defensive capabilities.

Game Design and Mechanics

My primary responsibility was the level design, characters, weapons, and various enemies, I created a variety of weapons that players could use to fend off the scavenger robots. The mechanics were designed to be intuitive yet challenging, requiring players to think on their feet and adapt to the increasing difficulty of the waves.

The Prince of Ibalong

About

A Capstone Project developed with my team 'StudioEepy', a third-person adventure game that follows the journey of Handyong, presented through a modern retelling of the Ibalong epic. The game integrates traditional Bicolano storytelling, engaging combat and with each chapter featuring a unique boss encounter.

Project Info

Team Size: 5
Time frame: 6 Months
Unity Engine (C#)

Game Overview

The Prince of Ibalong is a third-person adventure game developed as a capstone project with my team 'StudioEepy'. The game follows the journey of Handyong, presented through a modern retelling of the Ibalong epic. It integrates traditional Bicolano storytelling with engaging combat mechanics, with each chapter featuring a unique boss encounter.

Game Mechanics

The Prince of Ibalong features a dynamic combat system with a state machine-based approach, allowing for fluid transitions between different attack types and special moves. The game also includes a progression system that rewards players for completing objectives, defeating boss, and unlocking chapters Handyong's journey.
